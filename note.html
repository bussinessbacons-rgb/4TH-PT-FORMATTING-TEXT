Editor's Note

Artificial Intelligence has become one of the most powerful technologies in modern education. From smart tutoring systems to automated grading, AI is transforming the learning experience for both teachers and students. Schools across the world are beginning to integrate these tools into their classrooms.

However, this transformation also raises important questions. While AI can provide personalized learning and faster access to information, it may also reduce critical thinking if used irresponsibly. Students must learn how to use AI as a tool rather than a shortcut.
